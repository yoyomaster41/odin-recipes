Crispy Roasted Broccoli

Ingredients

  • 1 large head of broccoli
  • 3 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt

Recipe

  1. Preheat the oven to 425Â°F
  2. Put the broccoli on a baking sheet
  3. Drizzle broccoli with olive oil
  4. Sprinkle broccoli with salt
  5. Put broccoli in oven until lightly browned (14-18 minutes)
