World's Best Rice!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup rice
  • 1 1/2 cups water
  • 1 tablespoon of olive oil

Recipe

  1. Pour the rice into a saucepan
  2. Then, add the water and olive oil
  3. Bring the saucepan to a boil
  4. Reduce the heat to a simmer, and let the rice cook for 15 minutes
  5. Remove the saucepan from heat, and let the rice sit for 5 minutes before serving
