BBQ Tofu

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup BBQ sauce
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 pound extra-firm tofu

Recipe

  1. Dry the tofu out using a tofu press or paper towels
  2. Slice the tofu into cubes of your preferred size
  3. Marinade the tofu in soy sauce
  4. Heat a large skillet over medium and add oil
  5. Heat the oven to the highest broil
  6. Put the tofu in the skillet and cook until golden brown on each side
  7. Cover the bottom of a baking sheet with half of the BBQ sauce
  8. Add the tofu and cover it with the remaing BBQ sauce
  9. Put the tofu in the oven for 3 minutes on each side(6 minutes total)
  10. Remove from oven and serve
